Hide & Seek

Rule & Regulation of game

  • There is 3 level of game
    • Easy - 3 * 3
    • Medium - 4 * 4
    • Hard - 6 * 6
  • You have to find all three gift in minimum step to win the game.
  • If you open skull you will lose the game.
  • All the three Gift and skull will be hidden in random place.
  • If you try to open same gift twice it step will be counted in final score.
